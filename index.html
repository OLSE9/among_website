Among Us Helper



Among us discord bot that helps you moderate mutes of voice channel where you play among us, also able to store game codes

prefix: a!



VC = voice channel

Command Description Required permissions
a!start Start an among us game(mutes everyone in VC) Role "Among mod"
a!end End an among us game(Unmutes and revive everyone in VC) Role "Among mod"
a!vote Starts voting(Unmutes everyone in VC except dead people) Role "Among mod"
a!endvote Ends voting(Mutes everyone in VC and continues in game) Role "Among mod"
a!kill <user> Set someone as dead in game(He won't get unmuted during voting) Role "Among mod"
a!revive <user> Revive someone(Reverse of kill command) Role "Among mod"
a!leave Leave game and VC(You will get unmuted and disconnected from VC) -----
a!code [code] Set code for your voice channel, do not specify code to remove code from your voice channel -----
a!codes View codes that are set in your guild -----
a!help Get list of commands -----


Other features:

When user joines a channel with active among us game, he gets muted. When user moves from among us channel to another channel, he gets unmuted(not if he leaves, he must use a!leave command or move to another voice channel.)
